Artists and links to their work: 

- [Dawid Planeta](https://www.behance.net/dawidplaneta)
- [Simon Stålenhag](https://www.simonstalenhag.se/)
- [Zdzisław Beksiński](https://www.wikiart.org/en/zdislav-beksinski)
